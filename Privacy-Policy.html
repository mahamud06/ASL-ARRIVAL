Privacy Policy

Internal Proprietary Tool

The ASL Arrival Extension is a professional productivity tool developed exclusively for ASL BPO. It is designed to optimize product processing workflows on the Miinto platform by automating data extraction and performance tracking.

Privacy-First & Zero PII Policy This extension strictly avoids collecting personal information (PII). It does not access personal browsing history, emails, or files. All functions are built solely to make agent work more efficient and smooth.

1. Data Collection & Usage

As a mechanical necessity for workflow automation, the extension interacts with following non-personal data:

  • Product Attributes: Style IDs, Brands, Colors, and Categories are extracted for processing.
  • Activity Logs: Professional performance metrics (products processed per hour) are logged to internal Google Sheets.
  • Quality Control: Current URLs are checked to prevent duplicate entries and ensure data accuracy.

2. Authentication & Security

To maintain organizational integrity, the extension utilizes the user's Google Identity:

  • Access Control: Your Gmail address is used only to verify that you are an authorized employee of ASL BPO.
  • Permissions: Internal system flags determine if user-specific UI controls (Agent vs. Trainee) should be active.

3. Local Storage Handling

The extension uses local storage strictly for functional performance:

  • Efficiency Caching: Non-personal data is cached locally to reduce network lag and ensure a smooth experience.
  • Auto-Purge: A strict 7-day deletion policy is enforced to ensure no data remains in the browser long-term.

4. Host Scope

The extension is restricted to operate only on https://arrival.miinto.net/*. It remains completely inactive on all other websites to ensure your personal browsing is never affected.
